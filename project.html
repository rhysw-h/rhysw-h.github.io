How hosting the World Cup impacts tourism

I looked at whether there was an increase in tourism arrivals during the years of the world cup in countries that have hosted it over the past __ years.

I then looked at whether there was an increase in tourism expenditure during the years of the world cup in the same countries.

Conclusion for Tourism

We observed a positive trend in tourism arrivals in the years following the World Cup, suggesting that more tourists may have visited the host country during the event and continued to do so afterwards. Every host country in our analysis, with the exception of Russia, experienced an increase in tourist arrivals. Russia's deviation from this trend is likely attributable to its conflict with Ukraine during that period, which may have deterred visitors. Additionally, we noted an increase in tourism expenditure in three out of the five host countries examined. However, the increase was not substantial enough to attribute solely to the impact of hosting the World Cup.
